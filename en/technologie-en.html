Isn't the technology finally bad for artistic creators

2022, the technologic evolution become very consequent. It can be benefic to some activities but not for everyone

TECHNOLOGICAL ADVANCES YES…

Machinery creation allowed numerous progress in society. With gadgets that make everyday life easier, the effortless way to use social media and their duplication. Post content and sharing our work has never been this simple. Numerous practices have been enriched with the creation of digital illustrations, the setting up of streaming platforms for listening to music or reading online. You can have elementary access to your favorite contents. For creators, this is now possible to nimble practice with data processing software or fast colorization options for drawing. The audio recordings are in better quality, offer more sounds than ever before. Thanks to special effects, getting the viewer in the world of a film is very simple, in particular with 4D viewing.

BUT HOW MANY DOES IT COST ?

Things that get easier to make, it’s also stealing other people’s content. This is a real plight for artists. If formerly, by releasing an album or a book, it was absolutely necessary to get it materialistically, many contemporary artists see themselves losing the little money they can reap from copyright infringement. Currently, from the beginning of 2022, illustrators, graphists and more are fighting against the rise of artificial intelligence.

A LOSS IN THE VALUE OF MANUAL WORK

"What's the point of buying one of your drawings? Artificial intelligence can do the same thing for free.”

The work of a lot of artists lose its value. Many people find it absurd to pay for a free-lance artist in every domain. Because of new technologies, it is possible to enjoy things without spending money. Artificial intelligence and technological advances are to some extent replacing artists. Many no longer see the point of engaging professionally in an artistic concept.

L'étude de cas

Dans le cadre de cet article, quelques membres de l'équipe sont partis sur le terrain. Nous avons en effet pu poser cette question fâcheuse à des passants "Que pensez vous de l'intelligence artificielle?".
Les réponses ont été variées et les habitants de Montbéliard semblent, pour la plupart, d'avis plutôt négatif.
