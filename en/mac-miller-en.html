Come Back to Earth, an emblematic song by MAC MILLER

“ In my own way, this feel like livin'
Some alternate reality
And I was drownin', but now I'm swimmin'”

Malcolm James McCormick more commonly known as Mac Miller continues to resonate in the ears of his fans even 4 years after his disappearance, on september 7th, 2018.

SWIMMING, THE CONTINUITY OF THE SINGER’S STORY

Come back to earth, get the ball rolling of his sixth and last album Swimming. In this song, we find the melancholy as well in the lyrics as in the rhythm of the soundtrack. Start on a quiet note but, finally, strong in meaning isn’t it the best way to transport listeners into the artist’s world? “I’ll do anything to way out, of my head”, this is the last sentence of this song. On one hand it shows the proximity between the fans and the rapper. He clearly opens his heart about his mental health, as he often does. But, in one other hand, affects many people as we think. The way the words are phrased and the impact they have can obviously have a different effect on the way the words are heard. You can find a summary of the artist's discography in Youtuber SEB's video: LA TRISTE HISTOIRE DE MAC MILLER. The young producer knew how to hit the heart of people, by his philosophy.

« There is pressure. Many times in my life I've used that pressure to keep myself up and match the image I had of myself or the image others had of me. That's what creates pressure. [...] You have to have your own reality and that's what should be driving your life."

NOT ONLY A PRODUCER, BUT A SAVIOR

Today, one person on five people is affected by depression. Young people are more numerous than we think and a lot of them find peace in music. With Come Back to Earth and this album, Mac Miller's talks about serious topics but in a « pleasant » way. Listening to strangers talking about their situation is comforting. For many, Mac miller was a friend more than a simple producer

MAC MILLER AS A BRILLANT AND BEAUTIFUL PERSON

La créativité du rappeur se reflète dans ses 6 albums et autres mixtapes. K.I.D.S, Circles, GO.OD AM, Faces ou encore The divine féminine, des projets tous aussi intéressants que les autres par leur diversité musicale. Nombreuses sont les personnes qui restent touchées par son histoire. L’ambiance de chaque album est différente mais toujours aussi plaisante, un atout de l’artiste que nous regrettons à chaque écoute.

His creativity is reflected in his 6 albums and many mixtapes. KIDS, Circles, GO.OD AM, Faces or even The Divine Feminine are all as interesting as each other because of their musical diversity. The ambiance of each album is different but still pleasant. His character and his imagination remain assets that we regret every time we listen to him.
