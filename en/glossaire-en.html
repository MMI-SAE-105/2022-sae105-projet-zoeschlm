Glossaire

Reminiscence
The word reminiscence evokes a memory, often vague and hazy. It is similar to a feeling of nostalgia. It can be tied to a person or a moment of life.
Mixtape
The two terms mixtape and album should not be confused. The first one refers to a collection of songs usually stored on a CD or a tape. The mixtape is characterized by its lack (or almost) of blanks between the songs.
Burlesque
Burlesque describes a situation or atmosphere that is comical or even vulgar. Formerly a literary genre, it has nowadays become a cinematic style.
ArtBlock
This phenomenon corresponds to a lack of inspiration. Often used by the artists, this term is employed to talk about a person who wants to produce something but does not know what to do.
Impressionnism
Artistic movement that appeared in the XIXth century. It concerns pictorial artworks that seek to depict the moment. Based on observation and without any prior sketch, impressionist representations are often blurred.
