Better recognition for artistic content creators

Today, art is everywhere. On the street, in commercial relations, In differents hobbies and it benefits everyone in different ways. But behind these art pieces, less people think about their creators.

THE COST OF CREATION

Movies, music, books, posters, all require a lot of work that everyone ends up enjoying. Loving is something, but recognise a huge work is another. Even if they take a big place in society, art pieces aren’t valued in the working world. To be efficient, a certain notoriety has to be received for the project. You must know someone in the people around you who isn’t valued enough for his work, and you think that’s not fair. Today, It is difficult to artists to found a job on the long term that pays well.

ARTISTS AS PILLAR OF SOCIETY

Socially, they are able to pass a lot of messages. Street art is the most awarded in this case. Activism, support, through sculptures or graphs the support and contributions are multiple. Unfortunately, graph practice stays even today, prohibited by law, even if it isn't destroy a building. In music, numerous people who, with their songs, are talking softly to the young generation. Mac Miller, generation z’s artist, open his mind on social problems that more and more people are facing. Modern literature, more and more appreciated and recognized, begins slowly to be taught at school. Comics illustrate and help many people to understand some life situations

THE IMPORTANCE OF RECOGNITION

Currently in 2022, we don’t think enough about artists behind our favorite hobbies and activities. However, too low price receiving for artists is giving a give up feeling to the next generations to continue and achieve their projects. Projects are thought of with passion at the beginning. Helping your fellow man can be achieved by many ways, some more fun and impactives than others.
