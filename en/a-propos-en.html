About us

Thought for later project

Here, at the MMI department of Montbeliard, the Notes For Later project was given to us at the beginning of september. The goal was concretely to create a website for our future self. We only have some conditions about the article’s topics. We were then free to interpret it as we wished.

Reminiscence is concretely about my center's interests, at that time in 2022.

I saw the Notes for Later project as a tool to make a retrospective of my tastes for a few years. The items chosen are all personally important, from the music to the books mentioned.

As a student in first year of MMI, these articles reflect a part of me in this education. Touching several artistic practices, I always felt that their authors did not get much recognition.

I have chosen here to focus on the work behind each work, each project. By presenting these elements, it allows me to perhaps make some of them known.
