Wes Anderson, the contemporary painter of cinema

“Even though my films can sometimes seem a bit Byzantine and complex, all I want to do is tell a story in a conventional way.”

THE BUDAPEST HOTEL, A WEALTH OF CINEMATOGRAPHY

As a real visual slap, The Grand Budapest Hotel is awarded for the best films set Oscar, in 2015. Viewers ask themselves numerous questions by watching Wes Anderson plans. And these questions about his masterpiece out in 2015 are completely justified. Burlesque atmosphere for one hour and 40 minutes, the almost irreal temporality and the very kitch pink tint let the spectator drown in a no time experience.

A THOUGHFUL STORY

1932, we’re following Zero Mustafa, newly hired as Hotel Budapest’s lobby boy. At his side, Sir Gustave, funny, attached to his customers, the hotel concierge gets involved in a painting and inheritance story. Who can be the owner of this luxurious hotel? No one knows, this is the whole spot of the movie. Thought characters and scenery, the producer brings a certain part of the story to life. By prisonner’s clothes which remember of the Holocaust are involved the diplomatic relations between certain political leaders of the time. With a retrospective at the beginning and at the end of the movie, Zero Mustafa see himself being refused to travel in the train. He is beaten up by russian soldiers because of his ethnicity. A movie that is not so insignificant after all, since it seems to raise particular historical issues.

WES ANDERSON STRING SUCCESSFUL MOVIES

Today in 2022, he is aged 53. Wes Anderson stands out for his burlesque universe, which is sometimes difficult to pin down. An adaptation time is necessary before fully immersing oneself in its universe. If we don’t know about the story at the beginning, we are immediately seduced by his particular cinematographic style. And he doesn't stop there ! Lately, he released The French dispatch in cinemas. Filmed at Angoulême, it is strangely linked visually to the Budapest Hotel and Moonrise Kingdom released in 2012. In another style, in 2009, he made an animated movie by releasing The Fantastic Mr Fox. Most of Wes Anderson’s movies make a big hit at every release and we never cease to be immersed in a universe to breathe.
